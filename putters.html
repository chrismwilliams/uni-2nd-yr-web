Callaway Toulon Putter

Callaway Toulon Putter Images

Callaway Toulon Odyssey Indy Putter

The new Callaway Indianapolis is the premium line of putters Callaway currently releases. The company boasts that it's the highest MOI (Moment of Inertia) they have ever creted and you can expect it to carry a hefty price when it's released sometime in the spring.

The milled face, using Callaway's Deep Diamond Mill technology, is said to promote feedback to the player and promote a precision roll across a large part of the face.

The putter itself, inspired by Chip Ganassi and his racing team, has been created using 6061 aluminium on the face, 303 stainless steel on the sole, and carbon composite on the crown to help distribute the weight and improve forgiveness.

TaylorMade OS Spider Details

TaylorMade OS Spider

The TaylorMade OS Spider also boasts a large head to increase stability and create a larger sweet spot to help you make more putts.

Much like the Callaway before, the Spider's face has been milled to allow the face to grip the surface of the ball at impact for a better strike and improved roll and feel.

As you would expect from TaylorMade, the putter comes in many combinations of different lengths, avaiable for both left and right handed players. If your looking for a putter that offers the most forgiveness, this maybe the club for you.

TaylorMade OS Monte Carlo Details

TaylorMade OS Monte Carlo Images

TaylorMade OS Monte Carlo

The smaller less flashier cousin of the Spider is the TaylorMade Monte Carlo. The smaller profile, weighting 350g includes the easier-to-line-up red and white alignment aid following the colour scheme of TaylorMade.

The face boasts the same skin-milled finish for players who seek the stability of the larger mallet putter, but retaining the feel of a blade.

In terms of comparison to the Spider, it comes purly down to asthetics as they are almost identical in terms of performance and feedback. The Monte Carlo also follows the traditional look of a mallet and will appeal to players looking for an upgrade.

Titleist Scotty Cameron Futura 5CB Details

Titleist Scotty Cameron Futura 5CB

The Scotty Cameron name is synonymous with a premium putter and the Futura 5CB doesn't change that, the putter is an exquisitely hand crafted mid-mallet which includes a cavity to improve forgiveness.

Scotty Cameron's heel and toe stainless steel sole weighting philosophy is in effect here again, providing a putter that is both forgiving and stable throughout the putt.

The machined aluminium anodised black plate sets the tone of the putter and is certainly one of the best looking putter we've tested. If money isn't an issue, this is a big consideration for players looking for a new premium putter.

Titleist Scotty Cameron Futura 7M Details

Titleist Scotty Cameron Futura 7M Images

Titleist Scotty Cameron Futura 7M Putter

Much like the TaylorMade offering previous, the Scotty Cameron 7M putter is the bigger cousin of the 5CB, including the ability to change the weighted disc's fixed to the aluminium anodised back plate to customise to the players preference.

Where they do differ however is at address, with the 7M incorporating a sleek new bar back design to help you align those putts easier than ever.

In terms of construction, the Cameron offerings here both are constructed with 303 stainless steel for the head and aircraft 6061 aluminium for the face. This make-up allowed Cameron to position the weight further away from the face to make the club everymore forgiving.
