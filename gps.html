Garmin Approach G8 GPS Details

Garmin Approach G8 GPS Images

Garmin Approach G8 GPS

With a large colour 3in touchscreen loaded with over 40,000 courses from around the globe, the Garmin G8 is still the pocket caddy of choice. Not only does it include most of the courses your every likely to play, it also includes free updates which many other companies offer at a premium.

The G8 also works while wearing a glove, which means that you can set your target, manually place the pin, or enter your score into the digital scorecard while on the move. It also keeps track of how far you hit each club, providing you with all the tools you need to play your best

Garmin Approach S6 GPS Watch Details

Garmin Approach S6 GPS Watch Images

Garmin Approach S6 GPS Watch

Smartwatches are big at the moment, and the Garmin S6 has positioned itself as one of the premium golf GPS devices. With the premium price tag comes a high resolution colour touchscreen that includes many of the features its big brother includes, packaged in a wearable.

Additionally the S6 will also measure every swing you make, providing you with a metric of your tempo, providing you with instant feedback when you feel your swings not quite there. The device also connects to your phone, displaying your messages or emails as your walking the fairway

TomTom Golfer GPS Watch Details

TomTom Golfer GPS Watch Images

TomTom Golfer GPS Watch

The TomTom comes in at almost £100 cheeper than the Garmin S6, but the price difference doesn't tell the whole story. It may not have a colour screen or swing analysis, however it includes most of the features your average golfer needs.

The watch allows you to quickly find your yardage and enter your score with a large touch display. It also includes over 40,000 international courses which are updated regularly meaning its a serious contender for anyone looking for a smart device
