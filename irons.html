Technology

Featuring a newly design Exo-Cage head construction, the Callaway Big Bertha Irons provide more forgiveness and distance for all players. The addition of the Exo-Cage allows Callaway to distribute weight otherwise lost to other areas to provide these added benefits

Callaway Big Bertha OS Iron Review Details

LOOK & FEEL

As these irons are aimed at the higher handicapper, they are on the larger side. However unlike their predessor, the inclusion of the Exo-Cage has meant a significant reduction overall which results in a club that is both aesthetically pleasing whilst at the same time being forgiving for those misplaced shots

ACCURACY & FORGIVENESS

Being aimed very much in the game improver market, the Big Bertha's don't disappoint and are plenty forgiving with a larger sweet spot. The benefits of the Exo-Cage can be felt throughout the range of irons with the longer clubs a lot easier to hit and find the target

FINAL VERDICT

This really is a fantastic set and is a big consideration for anyone looking for a game-improver set. The new Exo-Cage isn't another one of those buzz words either with a tangible improvment in forgivness, especially in the longer irons which many amateurs stuggle with from time to time
